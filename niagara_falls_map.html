How to Use This Map
  • Search routes by name or number using the search bar.
  • Tap a route to highlight it on the map. Tap the map background to show all routes again.
  • Routes with two badges (103/203) have day and night/weekend options. Click each badge to see the exact route, sometimes they are different.
  • Dashed lines show interlined routes that connect end-to-end with the selected route.
  • Tap coloured dots on the map to see hub details and the routes serving them.
  • Tap Details → to visit a route’s full schedule page.
  • Use the moon icon for dark mode and the reset icon to reset the map view.
  • On mobile: swipe up on the bottom panel to browse routes.
